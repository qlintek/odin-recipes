Cracked Out Chicken Tater Tot Casserole

Filling Southern Casserole

This miserable world gives so many few pleasures. A wonderful partner, an obnoxious cat, and this delicious, filling casserole

Ingredients

  • 3 cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 16oz sour cream
  • 1 10.5oz can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 10z package dry Ranch dressing mix
  • 1 cup chopped cooked bacon
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 30 to 32oz bag frozen tater tots
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly spray a 9Ã—13-inch pan with cooking spray. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together chicken, sour cream, soup, Ranch mix, bacon and cheese. Fold in frozen tater tots. Spoon mixture into prepared pan.
  3. Bake uncovered for 40-45 minutes, until bubbly.
